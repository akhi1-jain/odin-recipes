About Us

Our Story:

The Garrison is not just a place to dine; it's a testament to our enduring spirit and unyielding ambition. In the heart of our beloved Birmingham, we've created a sanctuary that reflects the essence of the Peaky Blinders, where history and fine dining merge seamlessly.

The Peaky Blinders Touch:

Step inside, and you'll find yourself transported to the roaring streets of Small Heath, where our past meets the present. The ambiance is tinged with the same mystery and allure that surrounds our gang, and the menu is an ode to our audacious appetite for life.

Meet the Family:

Our team is a clan of passionate individuals, each bearing the indomitable spirit of the Blinders. From our talented chefs who wield their knives with the precision of a Shelby plan to our warm and attentive staff, we're a family that ensures you'll feel right at home, even if it's in the heart of our 'den.'

Why Choose The Garrison:

  • Unforgettable Flavors: Our dishes are an amalgamation of tradition and innovation, just like our illicit activities. Each bite tells a story of flavor, audacity, and defiance.
  • Authentic Ambiance: The Garrison isn't just a restaurant; it's a portal to our world. You'll find the same gritty charm and ambiance that define our era.
  • Warm Hospitality: As we say, "The Garrison is open to all." We welcome you to be a part of our world, if only for a night.

Join Us:

Whether you're a fellow Peaky Blinder or a traveler seeking adventure, The Garrison beckons. Come, raise a glass with us, savor our finest cuisine, and embark on a journey that promises an unforgettable night of revelry and indulgence.

The Garrison isn't just a dining experience; it's a declaration. We invite you to step into our world, where every moment is charged with history and every meal is a celebration of the Peaky Blinders' spirit.

By order of the Peaky Blinders, welcome to The Garrison.
