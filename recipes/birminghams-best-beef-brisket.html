Birmingham's Best Beef Brisket

"Savor the Tradition: Birmingham's Best Beef Brisket at The Garrison Tavern."

Indulge in a timeless favorite that pays homage to Birmingham's rich culinary heritage. Our mouthwatering beef brisket, slow-cooked to perfection, is a testament to tradition and flavor. Each tender slice is a journey through the heart of Birmingham—a taste of the very best.

Ingredients:

  • 1 beef brisket (5-6 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • Your favorite barbecue sauce

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Season the Brisket:
    • In a small bowl, combine kosher salt, black pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and brown sugar.
    • Rub the spice mixture evenly over the entire brisket. Let it sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat and Smoke:
    • Preheat your smoker to 225°F (110°C).
    • Place the brisket on the smoker grates and smoke for several hours, maintaining a consistent temperature. Use your favorite wood chips or chunks for added flavor.
  3. Low and Slow:
    • Smoke the brisket for 6-8 hours, or until it reaches an internal temperature of around 195°F (90°C).
    • During smoking, periodically spritz the brisket with beef broth to keep it moist.
  4. Wrap and Rest:
    • Remove the brisket from the smoker and wrap it in aluminum foil.
    • Let it rest for at least 30 minutes to an hour to allow the juices to redistribute.
  5. Slice and Serve:
    • Unwrap the brisket and slice it against the grain into thin, tender pieces.
    • Serve with your favorite barbecue sauce on the side.
